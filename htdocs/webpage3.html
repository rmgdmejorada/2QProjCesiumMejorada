The Senate of the Philippines

  • composed of twenty-four Senators
  • may serve for a maximum of two consecutive terms lasting for six years

Qualifications

  1. a natural-born citizen of the Philippines
  2. a registered voter
  3. must be able to read and write
  4. 35 years of age at the day of the election
  5. must have resided in the Philippines for not less than two years before the election is held
