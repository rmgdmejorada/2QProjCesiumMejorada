BOTO BENTE DOS

HOME PRESIDENTIAL VICE PRESIDENTIAL SENATORIAL TIPS

1

GET TO KNOW THE CANDIDATES

Research about the platforms, plans, and backgrounds of the candidates. Take advantage of news outlets, the internet, and other reliable sources of information available. Be mindful of fake information. Get to know the candidates responsibly.

2

EVALUATE THE CANDIDATES

To evaluate the candidates, these steps may be followed:

  1. Know the stands of the candidates on the issues that matter to you the most.
  2. With the data you have collected through researching about the candidates, examine how ready they are to take the position they are running for. Consider as well the campaign materials they utilized.
  3. Identify the opinions of other people on the candidates, as well as their reasons. Note, however, that this is only to help you in understanding your own opinions. Never allow anyone to dictate who you will vote as this takes away your right to choose.
  4. Listen to debates involving the candidates.

3

VOTE WHO THE COUNTRY NEEDS

The well-being of the country counts on your vote. Make sure to vote the candidates that the country needs.
