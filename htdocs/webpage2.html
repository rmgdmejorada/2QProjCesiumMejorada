The leading vice presidential candidates in the 2022 Philippine elections.

The Vice President of the Philippines

  • may serve for a term of six years
  • may run for reelection once

Qualifications

Article VII, Section 3 of the 1987 Constitution
  1. natural born Filipino
  2. a registered voter
  3. must be able to read and write
  4. 40 years of age at the day of the election
  5. must have resided in the Philippines ten years before the election is held

Duties

  1. may concurrently assume a cabinet position should the President of the Philippines offer the former one
  2. secretary concurrent to the position of vice president
  3. assume the presidency in case of the death, disability, or resignation of the incumbent President
